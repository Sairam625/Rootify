Insurance Claims Process

1

Report the Incident

Contact Rootify's disaster management team within 48 hours of the incident.

24/7 Helpline

Initial Documentation Needed:

  • Clear photographs of damaged crops
  • Video evidence of the damage
  • Date and time of the incident
  • Brief description of the damage
2

Assessment

Our field officers will visit your farm to assess the damage and prepare a detailed report.

Assessment Process:

Field officer assigned within 24 hours
Site visit scheduled within 48-72 hours
Detailed damage assessment conducted
Preliminary report generated
Damage estimation provided

Keep Ready:

  • Land ownership documents
  • Previous crop history
  • Insurance policy details
  • Recent farming receipts
3

Documentation

Submit required documents including land records, previous crop records, and proof of insurance premium payment.

Required Documents:

  • Land ownership documents
  • Previous crop history
  • Insurance policy details
  • Proof of insurance premium payment
4

Claim Processing

Our team processes your claim within 7-10 working days. Track your claim status through our mobile app or website.

Claim Processing Timeline:

Claim received within 48 hours
Assessment conducted within 72 hours
Claim processing initiated
Regular updates provided
Final settlement
5

Settlement

Once approved, claim amount is directly transferred to your registered bank account within 48 hours.

Settlement Timeline:

Approval within 7 working days
Funds transferred within 48 hours
